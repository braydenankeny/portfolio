Formula 1 is a racing series that has millions of fans around the globe. They race with special cars that are very low to the ground and are built entirely for speed. There are a total of 24 races in the 2024 Formula 1 Season. These races are held all over the world throughout the year. The race locations are: Bahrain, Saudi Arabia, Australia, Japan, China, Miami, Emilia-Romagna, Monaco, Canada, Spain, Austria, Great Britain, Hungary, Belgium,Netherlands, Italy, Azerbaijan, Singapore, Texas, Mexico, Brazil, Las Vegas, Qatar, and Abu Dhabi.
